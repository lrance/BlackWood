Follow these steps to create an advertisment in Blackwood

Step 1:

Click on the Advertisement button. The Ads window then appears.

Step 2:

Click on the New Ad button.

Step 3:

Select the desired media type, media sub-type, size, style, and if desired, a feature to highlight. After these selections are made, a preview of the ad will appear.

Step 4:

Click the Accept Ad or the Abort button.

Step 5:

Repeat the steps to create another ad or click the Done button.

REMEMBER: The ad will run until it is cancelled or there is no longer enough funds available to cover its cost.
